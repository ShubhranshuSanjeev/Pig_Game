Instrucions

  1. The game has two players, playing in rounds.

  2. In each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times as he whishes. Each result get added to his Round score.

  3. But, if the player rolls a 1, all his Round score gets lost. After that, it's the next player's turn.

  4. A player looses the entire score when he rolls two 6 in a row. After that it's next players turn.

  5. The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his Round score gets added to his Global score. After that, it's the next player's turn.

  6. The first player to reach 100 (default) points on Global score wins the game.
